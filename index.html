Who We Are

Teriaq Oncology Center

Teriaq Oncology Center offers integrated oncology services designed to guide and support patients at every stage of care. Supported by a network of experienced oncology physicians from across Egypt and leading medical schools, our team brings together diverse expertise to deliver coordinated, high-quality cancer care tailored to each patient's individual needs.

Our Approach to Care

Patient-Centered Care

Every patient is unique. That's why we develop individualized treatment plans that consider each patient's medical condition, personal needs, and overall well-being.

Evidence-Based Medicine

Our treatments follow the latest international oncology guidelines and medical research to ensure patients receive the highest standard of care and the best possible outcomes.

Multidisciplinary Expertise

We are proud to introduce Egypt's first oncology Consulto program, a collaborative platform where physicians from multiple oncology disciplines work together to provide comprehensive evaluation and personalized treatment planning.

Our Services

At Teriaq Oncology Center

We provide comprehensive oncology services designed to support patients through every stage of their care.

Chemotherapy

Modern chemotherapy protocols delivered in a safe, supportive setting.

Radiotherapy

Precision radiation therapy for targeted cancer treatment.

Radiation Treatment Using Advanced Imaging

Image-guided radiation for accuracy and better outcomes.

Bone Marrow Transplant

Specialized hemato-oncology and BMT care.

Pain Management

Expert support to manage pain and improve quality of life.

Psychological (Mental Health) Support

Counseling and mental health support for patients and families.

Nutrition Clinic

Tailored nutritional guidance during and after treatment.

Early Detection and Genetic Counseling

Screening and genetic counseling for at-risk individuals.

Pharmaceutical Services

Oncology pharmacy and medication management.

Recovery Program

Rehabilitation and recovery support after treatment.

Consulto (Multidisciplinary Consultation)

Egypt's first oncology Consulto: multiple specialists, one coordinated plan.

Home care

Professional nursing and supportive oncology care delivered in the comfort of your home.
